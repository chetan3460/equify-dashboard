# Analytics – Guide

<Callout type="note">Analytics (gauges, helpers) live under features/analytics.</Callout>

## Files
- GaugeChart.jsx – reusable gauge
- gauge-helpers.js – helpers for math/rendering
- analytic-cards-helpers.js – helpers for analytic summaries

## Patterns
- Keep helpers pure; keep rendering components declarative
- Accept props for thresholds/colors to avoid hard-coding

## Backend migration
- Most analytics read from mappers. Provide simple selectors/hooks for data sources.
